Turtles & Tortoises

Turtles and tortoises are not the same there is a difference. Turtles live in mostly water like a slider turtle, but a sea turtle would live in the ocean. Or tortoises, they live only on land.

Turtles are small reptiles that are cold-blooded. They are very endangered especially sea turtles. Sea turtles eat jelly fish but plastic bags look like them too! So sea turtles may swallow them by mistake and either become very sick or DIE.

Tortoises are large reptiles that are also cold-blooded! They are herbivores and have no teeth but they have a beak. They have claws for digging burrows and a big hard shell to hide in to escape predators.
